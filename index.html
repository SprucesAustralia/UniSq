AI Powered Cleaning Checklist

Smart solutions to keep your university clean and sanitary!

Classrooms

Standard Cleaning Tasks Done Comments
Wipe desks & chairs
Vacuum or sweep floors
Clean whiteboards & markers area
Empty trash bins
Disinfect door handles
Deep Cleaning Tasks Done Comments
Wipe desks & chairs thoroughly (including undersides)
Shampoo / steam clean carpets or floors
Clean walls & windows (interior)
Sanitize all electronic equipment (projectors, etc.)
Clean & disinfect window blinds / curtains
Dust & clean vents / ceiling fans

Top 2 Recommendations

  • Use low-odor disinfectants for a pleasant environment
  • Clean desk surfaces daily to reduce germs

2 Things to Avoid

  • Using the same cloth for desks and floors
  • Skipping high-touch items (light switches, doorknobs)

Offices

Standard Cleaning Tasks Done Comments
Dust desks & shelves
Vacuum / mop floors
Wipe telephones & electronics
Empty trash bins
Disinfect door handles
Deep Cleaning Tasks Done Comments
Thoroughly disinfect desktops & chairs
Clean windows (interior)
Polish wooden furniture
Sanitize keyboards & computer mice
Clean air vents & filters
Detail cleaning in corners & under furniture

Top 2 Recommendations

  • Encourage clean desk policies
  • Use eco-friendly cleaning products

2 Things to Avoid

  • Using one rag for all surfaces
  • Ignoring high-traffic areas (floors, entryways)

Restrooms

Standard Cleaning Tasks Done Comments
Clean & disinfect toilets, urinals
Wipe sinks & counters
Mop floors
Refill soap & paper dispensers
Empty sanitary bins
Deep Cleaning Tasks Done Comments
Descale toilets & urinals
Steam clean floors & wall tiles
Sanitize partitions & doors thoroughly
Clean vent covers & ceiling fixtures
Polish metal surfaces (faucets, etc.)
Check for mold in hidden corners

Top 2 Recommendations

  • Use color-coded cleaning tools for sanitary areas
  • Frequent disinfection on high-touch surfaces

2 Things to Avoid

  • Mixing harsh chemicals (dangerous fumes)
  • Skipping routine checks for supplies
