Standard Cleaning Tasks Done Comments
Wipe desks and chairs
Vacuum or sweep floors
Clean whiteboards & markers area
Empty trash bins
Disinfect door handles
Deep Cleaning Tasks Done Comments
Wipe desks/chairs thoroughly (undersides, legs)
Shampoo or steam floors/carpets
Clean walls & windows (interior)
Disinfect all electronic equipment (projectors, screens)
Dust & clean vents, fans, or AC units
Organize & wipe down supply cabinets

Top 2 Recommendations

  • Clean desk surfaces daily to reduce germs
  • Use low-odor disinfectants for a pleasant environment

2 Things to Avoid

  • Using the same cloth for floor and desk surfaces
  • Skipping high-touch items (light switches, doorknobs)

Disinfect desks & chairs thoroughly
Clean interior windows
Polish wooden furniture
Sanitize keyboards & computer mice
Clean vents & filters (if accessible)
Detailed corner & under-furniture vacuum

Top 2 Recommendations

  • Encourage clear desk policies
  • Use eco-friendly cleaning products

2 Things to Avoid

  • Using one rag for all surfaces
  • Ignoring high-traffic, high-touch areas

Standard Cleaning Tasks Done Comments
Clean & disinfect toilets, urinals
Wipe sinks & counters
Mop floors
Refill soap & paper dispensers
Empty sanitary bins
Deep Cleaning Tasks Done Comments
Descale toilets & urinals
Steam clean floors & wall tiles
Sanitize partitions & doors thoroughly
Clean vent covers & ceiling fixtures
Polish metal surfaces (faucets, etc.)
Check for mold & mildew in corners

Top 2 Recommendations

  • Use color-coded cleaning tools for restrooms
  • Keep daily logs to maintain cleanliness

2 Things to Avoid

  • Mixing strong chemicals (dangerous fumes)
  • Overlooking supply refills (soap, paper)
